# MA YYA Report

Implementation of the quarterly report required for Massachusetts Executive Office
of Health and Human Services (MA EOHHS) Youth and Young Adult (YYA) Homelessness
Demonstration Programs (YHDP).

## History
* Updated 10/2023 for the FY2024 changes
* Updated 12/2024 for the FY2025 changes
* Updated 8/2025 for the FY2026 changes

## Calculation Details

### A. Core Services
#### 1. Street Outreach / Collaborations
##### a. Unduplicated number of outreach contacts with YYA experiencing homelessness
Number of YYA who were either

Enrolled in a Street Outreach (SO) project
**or**
Had a referral source of Outreach Project (7) **and** a Current Living Situation of "homeless" as indicated by one of the following collected within the report range.
* 116 Place not meant for habitation (e.g., a vehicle,an abandoned building, bus/train/subway station/airport or anywhere outside)
* 101 Emergency shelter, including hotel or motel paid for with emergency voucher, or RHY-funded Host Home shelter
* 118 Safe Haven
* 302 Transitional housing for homeless persons (including homeless youth)
* 336 Staying or living in a friend's room, apartment, or house
* 335 Staying or living in a family member's room, apartment, or house

YYA with referral source of Outreach Project (7) **and** current living situation of any CLS that is not homeless (not in: 116, 101, 118, 302, 336, or 335.)

YYA enrolled within 365 days prior to report start date (except those in ES, SH, SO, or TH) with homeless CLS at entry (not: 116, 101, 118, 302, 336, or 335.)

##### e. Number of YYA who remain housed 1 year after receiving prevention services
